About Our School

Discover our rich history, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 1985, Amonto Girls Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence in Arua for over three decades. What started as a small community school has grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and personal growth in a Christian environment. We strive to develop well-rounded individuals who will make positive contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading Secondary school in Uganda, producing well-rounded students who excel academically and morally. We aim to set new standards in Secondary education while maintaining our core values and traditions.

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

We maintain high academic standards and encourage critical thinking.

Christian Values

Our foundation is built on strong Christian principles and moral values.

Community

We foster a strong sense of community and family involvement.

Character Development

We focus on developing strong character and leadership skills.
