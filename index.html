Welcome to Mother Mary Primary School

Dear Parents, Students, and Visitors,

Welcome to Mother Mary Primary School, where excellence meets compassion. For over three decades, we have been committed to providing quality education that nurtures not just academic achievement, but also character, creativity, and leadership skills.

Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where every child feels valued, supported, and encouraged to reach their full potential. We believe in holistic development that prepares our students not just for their next academic step, but for life.

As you explore our website, I invite you to discover the unique opportunities and rich traditions that make our school special.
