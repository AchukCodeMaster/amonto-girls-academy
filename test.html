Our Curriculum

Unity Secondary School follows the South Sudan National Curriculum, offering a wide range of subjects that prepare students for higher education and future careers.

Our O-Level curriculum (Senior 1 to Senior 4) provides a strong foundation in core subjects while allowing students to explore their interests through elective courses. The curriculum culminates in the South Sudan Certificate of Secondary Education (SSCSE) examinations.

Mathematics

Algebra, Geometry, Statistics, and Trigonometry

Sciences

Physics, Chemistry, and Biology

Languages

English, Arabic, and Indigenous Languages

Humanities

History, Geography, and Religious Studies

ICT

Computer Studies and Information Technology

Business Studies

Commerce, Accounting, and Entrepreneurship

Our A-Level curriculum (Senior 5 to Senior 6) offers specialized study paths that prepare students for university education. Students select three or four subjects based on their career aspirations and academic strengths.

Advanced Sciences

Physics, Chemistry, and Biology at advanced level

Advanced Mathematics

Pure Mathematics, Mechanics, and Statistics

Advanced Humanities

History, Geography, and Literature

Advanced Business

Economics, Accounting, and Business Management

Advanced ICT

Computer Science and Information Systems

Advanced Languages

English Literature and Language Studies

Academic Departments

Our school is organized into specialized departments, each led by experienced educators dedicated to excellence in their fields.

Science Department

Our Science Department fosters scientific inquiry and critical thinking through practical experiments and theoretical learning. Students develop a deep understanding of natural phenomena and scientific principles.

Physics Chemistry Biology Agricultural Science

Arts Department

The Arts Department cultivates creativity, critical analysis, and cultural awareness. Students explore literature, history, and social studies to develop a comprehensive understanding of human society and expression.

English Literature History Geography Religious Studies

Business Department

Our Business Department prepares students for the commercial world by teaching fundamental business principles, financial literacy, and entrepreneurial skills. Students learn to analyze markets and develop business strategies.

Commerce Accounting Economics Entrepreneurship

ICT Department

The ICT Department equips students with essential digital skills for the modern world. From basic computer literacy to advanced programming, students learn to harness technology for problem-solving and innovation.

Computer Studies Information Technology Programming Digital Media
